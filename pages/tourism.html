    • Open-air cinemas in Berlin

    Open-air cinema season in Berlin runs from May to September. Open-air cinemas in famous and hidden places offer a varied film program from cult films to the latest Hollywood films in the open air. All outdoor cinemas on the It is also the only OMU open-air cinema in the city. There is a different film every day this year, including highlights of the current cinema season, as well as classics and cult films. The open-air cinema is Berlin's only OmU open-air cinema and has been digitized for several seasons and thus promises visitors a significantly improved viewing experience. With films from all over the world, the Kreuzberg cinema on Mariannenplatz is cosmopolitan. As a rule, all films are shown in the original version with subtitles. A different film is shown every day, with German films showing English subtitles. Dogs may be brought on a leash.
